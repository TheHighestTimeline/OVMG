# NCNDA Sender — SOP

**Tool:** [ovmgncnda.netlify.app](https://ovmgncnda.netlify.app)
**Purpose:** Send a Mutual NCNDA to a counterparty in under 30 seconds from any device.
**Owner:** Tanner South

---

## When to use

Any time a counterparty needs to sign an NCNDA before further discussions — prospects from outreach, intro calls, deal-flow contacts, partner introductions.

## What you need before you start

- Counterparty's full name (or company name)
- Counterparty's email address
- The team password: **Imblessed**

## How to send

1. Open **ovmgncnda.netlify.app** (recommended: Add to Home Screen on iPhone — Share → Add to Home Screen)
2. **Your Email** — your OVMG email
3. **Password** — `Imblessed`
4. **Counterparty Name** — what you want on the document title (preview shows `[Name] X OVMG`)
5. **Counterparty Email** — where the signing request will go
6. **Notes** (optional) — context for the log: lead source, deal type, anything useful for future reference
7. Tap **Send NCNDA**

A green confirmation screen with the envelope ID means it's sent.

## What happens next (automated)

- Sent to Carsten first — he signs as OVMG
- Counterparty then receives the signing request
- Tanner and Carsten are CC'd on the fully executed PDF once both parties sign
- Every send is logged for audit and compliance purposes

## If anything goes wrong

Drop the error message (or a screenshot) in the OVMG group chat. Don't retry repeatedly — that creates duplicate envelopes the counterparty has to ignore.

## Rules

- Only use your own OVMG email as the sender
- Don't share the password outside the OVMG team
- You cannot sign on behalf of a counterparty under any circumstance
- Add a Note when context isn't obvious from the counterparty name alone
