§01 · Our Standard

Land, community,
and responsibility.

We are building a portfolio of data center opportunities that we are proud to put our names on, not just financially, but environmentally and socially. This is the standard we hold ourselves to from day one.

01

Land & Habitat Preservation

We evaluate every site with an eye toward minimizing ecological disruption. Where development and conservation can coexist, we build toward that outcome. Land is not a commodity; it is a responsibility we take seriously at the origination stage, before a single dollar is committed.

02

Community Uplift

Every data center we help bring to life has the potential to transform a community through local jobs, infrastructure investment, and long-term economic development. We build relationships with local stakeholders from the first site visit, not as an afterthought when permits are needed.

03

Responsible Resource Use

Data centers are resource-intensive by nature: power, water, land. We hold our sites and our partner network to a standard that accounts for long-term resource impact, and we make that part of the conversation at the deal stage, not after the fact.

04

Conservation-First Site Selection

Our founders' history in environmental work shapes how we screen and underwrite opportunities. Sites that create irrecoverable ecological harm don't make it into our portfolio. This isn't a policy we added later; it's the foundation the firm was built on.

BrightSunSolr
Strategic Power Partner

Converting stranded solar assets into clean, productive power infrastructure.

BrightSunSolr (founded by Justin Runnels, who also serves as OVMG's CTO) specializes in converting underutilized and stranded U.S. solar assets into productive infrastructure. SOLR ESS
Energy Storage Partner

Behind-the-meter battery storage enabling 24/7 baseload without grid dependency.

SOLR ESS (also led by Justin Runnels) delivers behind-the-meter battery energy storage systems purpose-built for data center infrastructure. By providing continuous, grid-independent baseload power, SOLR ESS eliminates one of the most significant constraints in data center site qualification: reliable, uninterrupted energy supply. The system is deployable across OVMG's full portfolio, including Kingsboro, Bennettsville, Millington, and Baton Rouge, and pairs directly with BrightSunSolr's solar generation layer for a fully integrated clean power stack.

Network Role
The storage layer that makes clean, continuous power a reality at every site in the OVMG portfolio.

Carbon Sponge
Sustainability Partner

Turbine-based CO2 capture that turns data center exhaust into a revenue stream and a net-zero credential.

Carbon Limit (founded by Tim Sperry, who also serves as OVMG's Climate Technology Advisor) deploys turbine-based carbon capture systems attached directly to data center exhaust streams. Carbon Limit transforms what is typically a liability — the emissions footprint of a large-scale data center — into an asset that improves both the environmental and financial profile of every site it touches.
