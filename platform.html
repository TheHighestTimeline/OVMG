§01 · What We Do

Not a developer.
A deal architect.

OVMG's value isn't in building; it's in connecting. We hold the sites, know the capital, and have the relationships to bring every party to the table and drive the transaction to close.

01 · Site Portfolio

Site Origination

We source and hold a curated portfolio of data center opportunities, greenfield, brownfield, and NTP-stage sites across multiple regions, each evaluated for power, location, and deal viability.

02 · Capital & Offtakers

The Right Network

We connect institutional capital and operators to the right sites at the right moment in the deal cycle. Our relationships span the full stack: equity, debt, hyperscalers, and colocation operators.

03 · Deal Process

From Deck to Close

We build the decks, organize the data rooms, and manage every step of the transaction with the discipline a capital partner expects, organized, credentialed, and always moving forward.

Five disciplines. One network. Every party a data center transaction requires.
